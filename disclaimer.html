Consult your doctor

Before beginning any fitness or nutrition program, you should consult a qualified healthcare provider — especially if you:

  • Have any pre-existing medical condition (heart disease, diabetes, hypertension, joint/bone issues, etc.)
  • Are currently taking prescription medication
  • Have a history of eating disorders
  • Have been sedentary for an extended period
  • Have experienced any recent injury or surgery
